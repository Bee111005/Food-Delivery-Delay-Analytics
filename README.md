# Food-Delivery-Delay-Analytics
Project Overview:-
This project analyzes food delivery operations to identify the primary factors contributing to delivery delays. Using Tableau, the analysis examines the impact of traffic conditions, weather, delivery distance, courier experience, vehicle type, and preparation time on overall delivery performance.

Business Problem:-
Food delivery companies need to minimize delays to improve customer satisfaction and operational efficiency.

This project aims to answer:
What factors contribute most to delivery delays?
How does traffic affect delivery performance?
Does weather significantly impact delivery time?
Do experienced couriers perform better?
Which vehicle types are most efficient?

Key Insights:-
Traffic Conditions
High traffic resulted in the longest average delivery times and was identified as the largest contributor to delays.
Distance Impact
Delivery times increased significantly for long-distance orders.
Courier Experience
Experienced couriers consistently completed deliveries faster than less experienced couriers.
Weather Conditions
Adverse weather conditions increased delivery times but had less impact than traffic congestion.
Operational Recommendation
Increase courier availability during high-traffic periods and optimize route allocation for long-distance deliveries.
